  Visit Duration Rate
Standard 25 min. $25/visit
Extended 40 min. $40/visit

The rates above are for walking a single dog. Please contact me for rates if you have more than one dog to walk.

Please note: During the summer months, I only offer daily walks during the morning hours. This is because the pavement reaches temperatures many degrees hotter than the air temperature. Dog pads are more sensitive than many people realize and walking a dog during the hottest part of the day puts them at risk for burned paw pads. In general, if it's too hot for you to walk barefoot, it's too hot for your dog as well.
