Pet Sitting
Standard visits are at least 20 minutes and include a potty break, feeding, administering medication (as needed) and lots of love!

Extended visits are 40 minutes and include everything you get with a standard visit, plus twice as many belly rubs.

Cats (and other self-sufficient pets) Only
I'm happy to make a quick 15 minute visit to look in on your more self-sufficientpets like cats, hamsters, gerbils, etc.

These visits include feeding, fresh water, socializing (as your pet wishes) and scooping the
litter box.

 For sitting clients, I will also bring in the mail and newspaper, water indoor plants, and rotate lights and shades to give your house a lived-in feel.

Dog Walking
Standard walks last at least 20 minutes and include lots of fresh air, ground sniffing and of course fresh water when we return home.

Extended walks are 40 minutes and twice the fun.
