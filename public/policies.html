Policies

Request Service

The easiest way to request service is to send an email to michelle@littlerascalsaustin.com with your name, address, phone number, pet info and the dates of service. Once your email is received, I'll give you a call to either set up a meet and greet (if you are a new client) or to discuss the details (if you are an existing client). New clients please allow at least two weeks when scheduling appointments. Existing clients please allow at least one weeks notice.

Meet and Greet

I require a face-to-face meeting with all new clients (human and animal). Before the meeting, all required forms will be emailed to you for you to complete. At the meet and greet, we will discuss the services you require, your pet's routine, and go over any questions you may have. This is also an important opportunity for your pet(s) and I to get to know one another. At this time, I will also take two copies of your house key and your deposit.

Payment

For pet sitting clients, a deposit of 50% is due when your keys are picked up. The remainder is due once your keys are returned to you after your trip.

For regular weekly walk clients, we can set up a payment schedule.

Cancellations

Unfortunately, paid deposits are not refundable.

Keys

I require two sets of your house keys. Key pick-up is free if given at the meet and greet. However, if a second trip is required to pick up the keys, there will be a $10 charge.

Unlike most pet sitters, I do not keep your keys on file after you return from your trip unless you are a frequent traveler. Once you return from your trip, your keys will be returned to you. There is no charge for key return.

Upon your Return

You must call or text to let me know you have returned home and your pets are back in your care. Otherwise I will continue to visit your pets as scheduled and you will be charged for those visits.

Vaccinations

All pets must be current on vaccinations.
