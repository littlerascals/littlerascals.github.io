Meet and Greet
I require a face-to-face meeting with all new clients (human and animal). Before the meeting, all required forms will be emailed to you. If these forms are completed before the meeting, there will be no charge for the meet and greet. However, if the forms are completed during the course of the meeting, there will be a charge of $20.

At the meet and greet, we will discuss the services you require, your pet's routine, and go over any questions you may have. This is also an important opportunity for your pet(s) and I to get to know one another. Please make sure all pets are present. At this time, I will also take two copies of your house key.

Request Service
New clients please allow at least two weeks when scheduling appointments. Existing clients please allow at least one weeks notice.

The easiest way to request service is to send an email to michelle@littlerascalsaustin.com with your name, address, phone number, pet info and the dates of service. Once your email is received, I'll give you a call to either set up a meet and greet (if you are a new client) or to discuss the details (if you are an existing client).

Payment
 Payment in full is required at the first visit. You may pay with cash or check.
Cancellations
Unfortunately, I am unable to offer refunds for canceled visits. However, you will receive credit for future service if the
appointment is canceled with at least 48 hours notice.

Last Minute Reservations
Emergencies happen and I will make every effort to accommodate you and your pets. Pet-sitting appointments scheduled less than 48 hours in advance will be charged an additional $30. Dog walking appointments scheduled less than 48 hours in advance will be charged an additional $10.

Keys
I require two sets of your house keys. Key pick-up is free if given at the meet and greet. However, if a second trip is required to pick up the keys, there will be a $10 charge.
